Proxima Centauri b

Proxima Centauri b is a potentially habitable exoplanet located 4.2 light-years from Earth. It orbits within the habitable zone of its star, which might allow for liquid water.

Kepler-452b

Kepler-452b is an exoplanet about 1,800 light-years away. It is 50% larger than Earth and orbits within the habitable zone of its star, similar to our Sun.

Kepler-1649c

Kepler-1649c is located 300 light-years away and is very similar to Earth in size and temperature, making it one of the most promising candidates for habitability.
